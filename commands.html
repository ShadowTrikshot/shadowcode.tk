Commands

Economy:
s!help - list of all the commands
s!start - starts progress
s!reset - resets progress
s!bal - your balance/money
s!buy - buy (item) (amount atleast 1)
s!daily - daily rewards by voting every 12 hours
s!give - give (amount of money) (user)
s!inv - things you bought
s!invite - bot invite link
s!lb - global leaderboard
s!profile - a little info about a user
s!shop - list of things you can buy

Fun/Misc:
s!meme - random meme from r/meme from reddit
s!ping - bots latency
